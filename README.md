# Surface Finance

Surface Finance brings institutional-grade yield strategies to memecoins — putting your idle tokens to work regardless of whether the market is going up, down, or sideways.

## What is Surface Finance?

Memecoins are defined by volatility. Most holders either speculate on price or sit idle waiting for the next move. Surface Finance offers a third option: put your tokens into a vault and let an actively managed strategy generate yield from that volatility itself.

The strategy runs with fully transparent accounting. You deposit your tokens, receive vault shares in return, and when the strategy generates returns, every share is worth more. When you want out, redeem your shares for the underlying token at the current value — no lock-up beyond the active trading window.

{% hint style="info" %}
Volatility is the one constant in crypto — bull market or bear market, it never goes away. Surface Finance is built to turn that constant into yield.
{% endhint %}

## How it works

1. **Deposit** — when the vault opens for deposits, send your tokens and receive vault shares at the current price.
2. **Strategy runs** — the vault deploys capital into its trading strategy. During this time, deposits and withdrawals are paused.
3. **Withdraw** — once the strategy concludes, the vault reopens. Withdraw at the updated value, or leave your shares in to roll over into the next round.

## Key properties

| | |
|---|---|
| Deposit token | Single ERC-20 token per vault |
| Your position | Vault shares — transferable, ERC-20 compatible |
| Strategy cadence | Epoch-based (funding → trading → withdrawal) |
| Access | Your shares are yours — held in your wallet, redeemable during withdrawal windows |
